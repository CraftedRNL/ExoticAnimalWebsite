About Bearded Dragons

  • Habitat

  • They live in arid forests and deserts, such as woodlands, coastal dunes, heathland, tropical savannahs.
  • Diet
  • Omnivore, insects, fruits and veggies. Main prey are Insects, Mice, and leaves
  • Family life
    • Bearded dragons aren't considered to be social animals. They are normally solitary and tend to be territorial.
  • Population
